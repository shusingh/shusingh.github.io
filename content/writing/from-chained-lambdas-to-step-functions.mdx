## The original architecture

Chained Lambdas with SNS hops. Where it broke as fan-out grew.

## What Step Functions changed

- Native parallelism via Map state
- Explicit retry/backoff
- One source of truth for execution history

## What we'd do differently next time

Lessons on payload sizing, idempotency, and observability.
